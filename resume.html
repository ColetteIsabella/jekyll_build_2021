Fircrest RHC

Attendant Counselor 2

At Fircrest, I work with people with Developmental Disabilities, helping them to achieve their highest level of independence. Some may never live away from a 24 hour care facility, but they are all unique and genuinely wonderful people and I am so grateful to get to work with them every day.
